Academee Educator's App Privacy Policy

Effective Date: Sept 10, 2024

At Academee Educators App, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we collect, how we use and safeguard this information, and the rights you have regarding your data when you use the Academee Educators App.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use the Academee Educators App:

  • Personal Information: When you sign up for an account, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, and contact details.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you use the app, including the features you access (e.g., scheme of works, eLesson notes, school calendars, exam questions, training videos, forums, and educational news).
  • Device Information: We collect data such as your device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
  • Location Information: We may collect location data if you grant us permission to access your device’s location services.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to provide, maintain, and improve the services offered by the Academee Educators App, including:

  • Personalizing your experience based on your preferences.
  • Providing access to schemes of work, eLesson notes, school calendars, and exam questions.
  • Enabling participation in forums and viewing educational news.
  • Delivering training videos and content tailored to your educational needs.
  • Sending you notifications about app updates, educational resources, or important news.
  • Responding to your requests, inquiries, and feedback.

Sharing of Information

We respect your privacy and do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following cases:

  • Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating the app, conducting our business, or serving users, as long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or government agency).
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your personal data may be transferred.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration. We implement security practices such as encryption, secure data storage, and access control to protect your personal information. However, please note that no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Your Data Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: You have the right to correct any inaccurate or incomplete information we have about you.
  • Deletion: You can request that we delete your personal data, subject to certain exceptions (e.g., legal obligations).
  • Withdrawal of Consent: You can withdraw your consent for us to collect or use your data at any time.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at academeeeducationalservices@gmail.com.

Third-Party Links and Services

The Academee Educators App may contain links to third-party websites or services, such as external resources for educators. This Privacy Policy does not apply to third-party services. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of any third-party services you access through the app.

Children’s Privacy

The Academee Educators App is intended for use by educators and school administrators. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child, please contact us so that we can take appropriate action.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated effective date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
